Martin Paulus, M.D. and his collaborators at the Laureate Institute for Brain Research (LIBR) are conducting a research study to 1) evaluate the feasibility of collecting data from online psychological tasks completed on Amazon Mechanical Turk (MTurk) and 2) evaluate the test-retest reliability of tasks completed on MTurk. Approximately 200 people will participate in the study.

If you agree to be in this study, you will complete two sessions:

Session 1: (~30 minutes)

Part A: You will complete 4 Surveys that ask questions about your demographic information and psychological well-being.

  1. Demographic Information
  2. Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-8)
  3. Overall Anxiety Severity and Impairment Scale (OASIS)
  4. Anxiety Sensitiviy Index (ASI-3)

Part B: You will complete the Dot Probe Task:

During the task, you will be shown images on the screen and presented with a red bar before and after each image. Using your keyboard, you will be asked to determine if the orientation of the bars was the same or different. The exact instructions will be provided to you when you reach this portion of the human intelligence task (HIT).

The images are from a standard set made for psychological experiments. Some of the images may contain graphical content.

Session 2: (~30 minutes, completed at least 24 hours after Session 1)

For session 2, you will complete the same survey and task as session 1.

If you provide an email address, we will remind you when 24 hours have passed and it is time to complete Session 2. After Session 2 is complete, you will be given a code to for a $3 reward for your time and effort on Mechanical Turk. 

Participation in research is voluntary. You may stop this HIT at any time when you feel uncomfortable. If you have any questions, please contact LIBR at jtouthang@libr.net.

No personally identifiable information will be collected in this study except your email address if provided. If you do provide an email address, we will delete it from the database once you have completed session 2!

You must complete both sessions to receive payment.



The email you provide will be deleted from the database once you have completed session 2
